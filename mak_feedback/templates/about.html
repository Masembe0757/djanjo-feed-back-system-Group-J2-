System Description

The Makerere Student Feedback System is an online platform designed to collect valuable feedback from students about their educational experiences at Makerere University. We believe that your feedback is crucial in improving the quality of education and enhancing the learning environment for all of the students.

About us

Mission and Vision

Mission: To create an efficient and user-friendly platform for students to share their feedback and contribute to the continuous improvement of the great Makerere University.

Vision: To foster a collaborative feedback culture that empowers students to have a voice and drives positive changes across the university community.

Key Features

  • Provide feedback on courses, instructors, and campus facilities.
  • Anonymous feedback submission to ensure privacy and encourage honest responses.
  • Regular analysis of feedback to identify areas for improvement and implement necessary changes.
